🔥 Trending Now
Before/After Transformation — The Proof Formula
Split-screen before/after content consistently outperforms all other formats in this niche. Viewers stop scrolling because of the visual contrast. The key is showing a real, believable transformation within the first 2 seconds.
Image Prompt →
A striking split-screen promotional image. Left side: dull, tired-looking skin with visible concerns. Right side: the same woman with glowing, radiant skin holding the product. Text overlay: "14 days later". Luxury bathroom setting, warm lighting, magazine quality.
Instagram Reels
📈 High Engagement
The "I Tried It So You Don't Have To" — Honest Review
Authentic, honest review-style content builds massive trust. The audience feels like they're getting insider knowledge. This format drives high save rates and direct purchase intent, especially when the creator seems relatable.
Image Prompt →
Candid lifestyle photo of a real-looking woman in her 30s, casual home setting, holding the product and looking directly at camera with an honest, warm expression. Natural lighting, slightly imperfect — feels real not staged. Text: "My honest thoughts after 30 days".

💰 High ROAS
The Problem/Solution — Direct Response Formula
Direct response ads that lead with the problem outperform lifestyle ads 3:1 on Facebook. The audience immediately self-identifies with the problem, making them highly receptive to the solution. Works best with a clear, specific claim.
Image Prompt →
Professional advertising photo split into two panels. Top panel: frustrated woman looking at skin concern in mirror, muted colours. Bottom panel: same woman smiling confidently, holding the product, vibrant colours. Bold text overlay: "Finally. Something that actually works." Premium quality, 1:1 format for Facebook feed.
